1. **Fix the 2 errors** — remove `priority` from the free-guide image (or, if it's that page's LCP, add `app/free-guide/page.tsx` to the ESLint ignore-list for the `<Image priority>` rule), and change `let timeout` → `const timeout` in SectionScroller.
